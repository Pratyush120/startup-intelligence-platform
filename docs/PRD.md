# Strategic Decision Intelligence Platform

## Vision

An AI-powered platform that continuously monitors the Indian startup ecosystem and converts fragmented public information into structured business intelligence for investors, recruiters, consultants, corporate strategy teams, and founders.

---

## Problem Statement

Every day, thousands of startup-related updates are published across news websites, startup directories, blogs, funding announcements, product launch platforms, company websites, and social media.

This information is:

- scattered
- inconsistent
- duplicated
- difficult to analyze
- impossible to monitor manually

Decision-makers spend significant time gathering information before they can begin analyzing it.

---

## Solution

The Strategic Decision Intelligence Platform automatically

- collects startup information
- validates it
- removes duplicates
- extracts business events
- identifies companies
- links events together
- calculates business KPIs
- generates executive summaries
- visualizes trends

The platform transforms unstructured news into actionable business intelligence.
